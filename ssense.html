Summary
SSENSE redesign is a proposal that aims to solve its current website priority issue, to broaden its users' luxury selection range and at the same time to promote a more useful and engaging creative shopping environment amongst its users.
Background
SSENSE is a fashion platform based on Canada that sells products from luxury and street-wear designers. Also, they produces its own original editorial content.
The primary mission of Ssense is e-commerce, sells luxury goods. The secondary mission should be the editorial content, give customers some information about fashion industry latest trend.
